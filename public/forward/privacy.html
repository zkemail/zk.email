Keyword Forwarder

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Keyword Forwarder (“the add-on,” “we,” “us”) is a Gmail add-on that searches your mailbox for messages matching rules you configure and forwards those messages to addresses you choose. This policy describes what data is involved and how it is handled.

1. Who processes your data

When you install and authorize the add-on, Google Apps Script runs as you against your Gmail account. Forwarding and search happen through Google’s APIs under your OAuth consent. We do not operate a separate mail server that stores your inbox.

2. Information we access

With your permission, the add-on may:

  • Read Gmail messages and metadata needed to match your keyword or search query.
  • Forward matching messages to destinations you configure, and apply labels so the same thread is not forwarded twice.
  • Read your Google account email address to show billing status and prefill checkout.
  • Create time-based triggers in your Apps Script project to run on the schedule you select.

3. Information we store

Stored in Google’s Apps Script property stores (tied to your account / script):

  • Your automations (keyword/query, destination, enabled state, run stats).
  • Your preferred check schedule (5 minutes, hourly, or daily).
  • Monthly free-tier usage counts.
  • Paid-plan status keyed by email address when Stripe confirms a subscription (processed by our webhook).

Stripe processes payments. We receive subscription and customer email events needed to unlock Pro; card details stay with Stripe.

4. How we use information

Only to provide the forwarding service, enforce free/Pro limits, and operate billing. We do not sell your email content or sell personal data.

5. Sharing

Message content is shared with the forward-to addresses you configure (that is the product). Payment data is handled by Stripe. Hosting for this website may be provided by ZK Email / our deployment provider. Otherwise we do not share personal data except as required by law.

6. Retention & deletion

Automation settings and usage data remain until you delete them, uninstall the add-on, or clear Apps Script properties. To stop processing, disable or delete automations and uninstall the add-on from Gmail. Contact us if you need help removing a Pro subscription record.

7. Security

Access relies on Google OAuth scopes you grant. Keep your Google account secure. Review scopes in the Google account permissions page anytime.

8. Children

The add-on is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy. The effective date above will change when we revise it.

10. Contact

Questions: GitHub issues or via zk.email.
